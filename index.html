Profile ╖ Recruiter Brief

Full stack developer building clear, reliable AI-powered web products.

" I turn complex ideas into products people can understand and use. "

I am a full stack developer focused on building practical web applications with clean interfaces, strong backend logic, and AI features that solve real user problems. My work combines React and Next.js on the frontend with Node.js, Python, APIs, and database design on the backend.

I enjoy taking complicated workflows and making them simple. In PolicyAI, that means helping users understand insurance documents through summaries, risk detection, and clear answers. In the Spotify Clone, it means designing a familiar music experience with smooth navigation and structured full-stack logic.

Recruiters can expect someone who is comfortable learning quickly, writing organized code, debugging carefully, and improving the user experience until the product feels polished and reliable.
